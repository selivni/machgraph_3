h = step forward
j = step backwards
m = capture/release mouse
a = toggle multisampling on/off
w = toggle wind on/off
shift+w = strengthen the wind (starts decreasing rapidly, press multiple times for visible effect)
r = toggle wind on/off, random strength
d = debug mode (don't remember what it does anymore, probably writes stuff in console)
arrow keys = rotate camera (if you have no mouse/your rigth hand is busy)
